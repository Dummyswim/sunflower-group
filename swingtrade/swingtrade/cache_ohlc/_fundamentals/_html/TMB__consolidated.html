About

Incorporated in 1921, Tamilnad Mercantile Bank (TMB) is one of the oldest private sector banks in India. It offers an array of banking and financial services to retail customers, micro, small, and medium enterprises (MSMEs).[1]

Key Points

Leading Private sector Bank
Tamilnad Mercantile Bank Limited (“TMB”) is a 100-year-old leading private sector bank in India. [1] [2] As of Q1FY26, it has a branch network of 754 branches (including 169 correspondents) of which 47% are located in semi-urban areas, 16% in urban, 22% in rural, and 15% in metropolitan centers. The bank has an ATM network of 1,150.[3]
