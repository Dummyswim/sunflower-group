About

Incorporated in 2012, Tracxn Technologies
Ltd offers Tracxn 'Platform' which tracks and curates data of startups, on a subscription basis[1]

Key Points

Business Overview:[1]
The company operates ''Tracxn'' , a market intelligence platform that provides data and software for the global private markets. It offers a subscription-based platform to provide comprehensive private company data, including transactions, financials, shareholding, valuations for deal sourcing, M&A opportunities, deal diligence, private markets analysis, and tracking emerging themes. Tracxn tracks over 4.0 million entities, 72,900+ investor profiles, and 7,86,000+ transactions.[2]
