About

Incorporated in 2006, Plaza Wires Ltd manufactures and trades electrical wire
and allied product[1]

Key Points

Business Overview:[1]
PWL is an ISO 9001:2015 and 14001:2015 certified company in the business of manufacturing and selling of wires, and selling and marketing of LT aluminium cables and fast moving electrical goods under its flagship brand Plaza Cables, and home brands such as Action Wires and PCG. Company's product mix comprises different type of wires and cables, and fastmoving electrical goods such as electric fans, water heaters, switches and switchgears, Poly Vinyl Chloride insulated electrical tape and Poly Vinyl Chloride conduit pipe & accessories.
