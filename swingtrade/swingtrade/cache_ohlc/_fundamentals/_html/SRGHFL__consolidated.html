About

Incorporated in 1999, SRG Housing Ltd provides loans to Retail customers for construction, repair, renovation or purchase of residential property, and loans against property[1]

Key Points

Business Overview:[1][2]
SRG Housing Finance is a leading rural housing finance company, focusing on New-to-Credit, Underserved & Unorganized population. They offer small ticket loans, tailored products, and simplified access to finance through minimal documentation and fast processing. As of FY25, they operated 90 branches across 7 states with an AUM of ₹759 crore
