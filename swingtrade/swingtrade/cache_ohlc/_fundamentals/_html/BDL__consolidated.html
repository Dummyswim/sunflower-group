About

Bharat Dynamics (BDL), is a Government of India Enterprise. It is engaged in the manufacturing of guided missiles and allied defence equipments [1] [2].

Key Points

Product Portfolio
The company offers a comprehensive defence portfolio including Surface-to-Air Missiles, Air-to-Air Missiles, Anti-Tank Guided Missiles, torpedoes, and launchers. It also offers counter-measure systems and specialised test equipment for missile and underwater weapon platforms. [1]
